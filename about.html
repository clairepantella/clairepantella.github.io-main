Claire Pantella: CIA Illustration Freshman

Claire Pantella is a native of Bloomsburg Pennsylvania, currently studying illustration at the Cleveland Institute of Art, in Cleveland Ohio. Her passion for art stems from her childhood fascination with watching Disney animated films to investigate their extraordinary characters and fantastical worlds. After dabbling in business, she decided to chase her dream of attending art school and pursuing a career as a professional artist.

Her works are inspired by the subtle art of storytelling and experimentation. Beyond the studio, Claire participates in many community projects and youth art education programs. She enjoys spending time with her two feathered friends, Arty and Doodle, who have inspired and motivated her for several years.

As a Freshman at CIA, Claire is focusing on expanding her portfolio of both digital and traditional artwork. With backgrounds in drawing, painting, and sculpture, her skills have grown exponentially. Claire hopes to continue her journey and one-day work for the Walt Disney Company, creating the same films she adored as a child and spreading the magic.
